Watermelon, Cucumber, and Mint Salad

    Ingredient:
  • 1 large seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 12 leaves fresh mint, thinly sliced
    Steps:
  1. Gently mix watermelon, tomatoes, feta cheese, onion, and mint in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl; drizzle over salad and gently toss to coat.

This watermelon tomato salad with feta cheese is the perfect summer salad!

It's delicious with grilled meat on a hot day. For the best results, dress the salad right before you eat it and serve it at room temperature.
